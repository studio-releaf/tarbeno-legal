Tarbeno – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Summary: Tarbeno does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your own device.

1. Data Collection

Tarbeno requires no account, registration, or login. The app does not transmit any personal data over the internet. All data — including exam dates, topic progress, mock test results, and targets — is stored only in your device's local storage.

2. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

3. Purchases

In-app purchases (Tarbeno Premium) are processed by Google Play. Your payment information is handled by Google Play; Tarbeno has no access to it. See the Google Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Backup

The backup feature exports your data as a file to a location you choose. Storing and sharing this file is entirely under your control; Tarbeno has no access to it.

5. Children's Privacy

Since Tarbeno collects no personal data, it collects no data from children either.

6. Changes

If this policy is updated, changes will be published on this page.

7. Contact

For questions: ataseversoftware@gmail.com
